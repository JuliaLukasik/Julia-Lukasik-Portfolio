About me
Graduating this May from the University of Wisconsin Milwaukee with a Bachelor of Business Administration in Marketing, I've spent the past few years building a strong foundation in both creative and strategic work.

I'm drawn to all kinds of marketing design: brand visuals, social graphics, photography, layout work, anything that blends creativity with strategy. Over the past three years, I've worked in the construction industry, first as a proposals intern and then as a marketing intern. That experience taught me how to work within brand guidelines, collaborate across teams, and understand everything that comes with corporate marketing.

Outside of design, coaching volleyball at Shorewood became one of the most meaningful parts of my college experience. Working with both middle school and high school athletes taught me patience, leadership, and how to show up for people, which enhances my everyday work.
